My Story

I spent over a decade in the financial sector working in compliance, AML/KYC, brokerage operations, client onboarding, and process improvement. In those roles, I learned how to solve problems in complex, high-stakes environments and how important reliable systems are to both businesses and users. Over time, I realized I wanted to do more than work alongside technology. I wanted to build it.

That led me to make a career pivot and enroll in the Computer Programming and Analysis advanced diploma at George Brown Polytechnic, where I am developing hands-on skills in Python, Java, JavaScript, PHP, SQL, and C#. Today, I am combining my background in finance, operations, and systems thinking with software development to pursue what I have always truly wanted: a career as a software engineer.
